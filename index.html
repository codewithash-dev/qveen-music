Hi, I'm Qveen

a Nigerian born multi-hyphenate living in the heart of London's vibrant creative scene. Trained as an architect, noxz has seamlessly blended his design acumen with his musical prowess. His journey began in childhood, with early explorations in music that saw him playing multiple instruments as a kid. This musical foundation paralleled his architectural pursuits, leading him to prestigious roles, including a tenure at Zaha Hadid Architects. However, the global pause during the lockdowns became a catalyst for noxz to share his musical creations online, rapidly building a global community of 800,000+ people across all platforms. This momentum culminated in the release of his beat tapes, that have gained a combined listen of 10M+ plays.

noxz's creative process is deeply influenced by his surroundings. He believes that environment and emotion are integral to his art. In both his architectural designs and musical compositions, noxz strives to evoke powerful emotional responses, creating spaces and sounds that linger in the minds and hearts of his audience. This philosophy is evident in his collaborative works, such as the sultry R&B track "When You're Around" with South African singer Lusanda, which originated from a spontaneous TikTok interaction.

"He knows how to invoke a feeling"
Spotify UK

Beyond original productions, Noxz has gained acclaim for his inventive mashups, which have collectively amassed millions of streams across SoundCloud and YouTube. These mashups showcase his ability to intertwine diverse genres, creating a fresh, immersive auditory language.

As he continues to explore and expand the boundaries of these intertwined disciplines, noxz stands as a testament to the harmonious fusion of space and sound.
